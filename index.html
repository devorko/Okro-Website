About Us
Okro is a weekly subscription service for fruits and vegetables that are delivered at your doorstep, within 24 hours of harvest, for the first time in India. The harvest details of every item in the basket are updated in the app itself for you to check. The simple and elegant interface helps you order and manage your weekly basket effortlessly.

Okro is started by friends who have graduated from BITS, ESSEC, IIM & USC. The sole purpose & mission behind founders’ passion: Make your hard work worth it.

You work so hard to provide everything to your family and your so loved children. What is it all for if we can’t give them good and healthy food ? The sole purpose of Okro is to help you provide pure, fresh & healthy food to your children for which you have put in your blood, sweat and tears.
